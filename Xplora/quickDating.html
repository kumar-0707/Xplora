Quick Outing

Explore nearby treasures hassle-free! Our quick tours are designed for your spur-of-the-moment cravings for excitement. Just grab your essentials, leave the logistics to us, and get ready for unforgettable experiences. Let the adventure begin!

VELLORE

Discover the rich heritage of Vellore, India, with its historic forts and temples. Explore the grandeur of the 16th-century Vellore Fort, a marvel of Dravidian architecture. Immerse yourself in spirituality at the golden-domed Jalakandeswarar Temple. Indulge in local cuisine and vibrant markets, making memories to cherish in this cultural gem.

PUDUCHERRY

Puducherry's blend of French elegance and Indian vibrancy. Wander the charming streets, bask in the sun at Promenade Beach, and find serenity at Aurobindo Ashram. Experience the unique fusion of cultures in this coastal gem.

NILGIRIS

Nilgiris beckons with its lush tea estates, misty mountains, and serene lakes. Explore the colonial charm of Ooty, ride the scenic Nilgiri Mountain Railway, and indulge in freshly brewed tea. Trek through the verdant hills of Coonoor or take a boat ride on the emerald waters of Ooty Lake

TIRUVANNAMALAI

A spiritual haven in Tamil Nadu, invites you to immerse yourself in its sacred aura. Marvel at the majestic Arunachaleswarar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and witness the grandeur of its towering gopurams. Embark on a spiritual journey with a circumambulation (Girivalam) around the holy Arunachala Hill.
